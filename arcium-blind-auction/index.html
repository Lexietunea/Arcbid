How It Works

Four steps. Fully private.

Traditional on-chain auctions expose every bid to every validator in real time, enabling front-running and MEV exploitation. ArcBid uses Arcium MXE to make the entire bidding process cryptographically sealed.

0x7f3a2c91... ARC-MXE-SEALED ████ ██ █████
01

Encrypt Your Bid

Before your transaction is signed, your bid amount is encrypted by the Arcium MXE cluster using a distributed key. The Solana program receives only ciphertext — your amount is never exposed, not even to validators.

02

Auction Runs Blind

All sealed bids sit on-chain as ciphertext throughout the auction window. No participant, validator, or block explorer can observe any bid amount. Front-running is structurally impossible.

03

MXE Computes the Winner

When the auction closes, Arcium MXE nodes perform multi-party comparison over all encrypted bids. No single node holds the full decryption key. Only the winner and price are output, with a ZK-SNARK proof of correct computation.

04

On-Chain Settlement

The Anchor smart contract verifies the ZK proof, transfers the asset to the winner, and automatically refunds all other deposits — trustlessly, with a full on-chain audit trail. Losing bids are never revealed.

Use Cases

Built for fairness,
not opacity.

ArcBid is an open protocol. The smart contract is public. The encryption is auditable. The outcome is ZK-provable.

I

NFT Drops

Launch NFT collections where every buyer competes on equal footing. No bots, no snipers, no insider advantages. The market sets the price fairly.

II

DAO Treasury Sales

DAOs can liquidate treasury assets without revealing floor prices or triggering front-running. Maximum value extraction, minimum information leakage.

III

Token Distributions

Allocate token lots to the highest bidders without exposing allocation sizes mid-auction. True price discovery for newly launched protocols.

IV

Private Liquidations

Liquidate collateral assets without triggering panic or allowing bots to exploit liquidation events. Sealed, private, mathematically fair.
